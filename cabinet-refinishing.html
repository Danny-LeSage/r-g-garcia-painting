Cabinet Refinishing

Update the look of your cabinets without taking on a full remodel.

Get Free Estimate

A fresh cabinet look without full replacement

Cabinet refinishing can completely change the feel of a kitchen, bathroom, or built-in space without the cost and disruption of tearing everything out. It is one of the biggest visual updates you can make while keeping what already works.

We focus on the prep, product choice, and finish quality that make cabinets feel clean, updated, and durable instead of rushed or temporary.

What we pay attention to

  • ✓ Thorough cleaning, prep, and surface attention before refinishing begins
  • ✓ Color and finish guidance to help the update match the rest of your home
  • ✓ A cleaner, more cost-conscious option than replacing usable cabinets
  • ✓ Practical scheduling and communication so you know what to expect during the project
  • ✓ Durable finishes designed for everyday use in busy rooms

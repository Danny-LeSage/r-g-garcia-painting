About R&G Garcia Painting

Professional painting services built on trust, quality, and craftsmanship

Our Story

R&G Garcia Painting is more than just a painting company. It is a family story built on hard work, trust, and pride in doing things the right way.

Elmer immigrated to the United States from Guatemala in 2014 to marry his wife, Natalie. In 2015, he began his painting career with a local company, where he developed the skills and attention to detail that still define his work today.

When Elmer and Natalie were expecting their second child, they decided to take a leap of faith and start their own business. They wanted more than just a job. They wanted the flexibility to be present for their family and to build something meaningful for their children, Ryan and Gianna. That’s how R&G Garcia Painting was born.

From the very beginning, the mission has been simple: deliver high-quality work and treat every customer with honesty and respect. As a family-owned business, we understand how important your home is. That is why we treat every project as if we were working on our own home with care, cleanliness, and attention to every detail.

For Elmer, painting is not just work; it is a craft. He is a true perfectionist who takes pride in every finish, every line, and every detail. Our goal is simple: when the job is done, you should love the result.

We have built our business one satisfied customer at a time. Most of our work comes from word of mouth and referrals, which means everything to us. It is a reflection of the trust our customers place in us and a standard we work hard to uphold on every project.

Elmer brings strong values from his Guatemalan roots. Hard work, integrity, and pride in doing the job right the first time. Those values are at the core of everything we do.

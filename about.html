About R&G Garcia Painting

A family story built on hard work, trust, and pride in doing things the right way.

Our Story

R&G Garcia Painting is a family-owned company built on hard work, respect, and pride in our craft. We bring a perfectionist's attention to detail to every home, treating each project with the care it deserves.

Elmer immigrated to the United States from Guatemala in 2014 when he married Natalie. He began his painting career in 2015 working for a local company, where he built the hands-on experience and eye for detail that still define his work today.

When Elmer and Natalie were expecting their second child, they decided to take a leap of faith and start their own business. That is how R&G Garcia Painting was born, named for their son Ryan and daughter Gianna.

From the beginning, the goal has been simple: deliver high-quality work and treat every customer with honesty and respect. We know your home matters, so we approach every project with care, cleanliness, and attention to detail.

Painting is not just work for Elmer. It is a craft. He takes pride in every finish, every line, and every detail, and our goal is always the same: when the job is done, you should truly love the result.

We have grown one customer at a time, mostly through repeat business and word of mouth. That trust means everything to our family, and it is what pushes us to show up with the same care on every single project.

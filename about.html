About R&G Garcia Painting

Professional painting services built on trust, quality, and craftsmanship

Our Story

R&G Garcia Painting was founded on a simple belief: quality craftsmanship and honest customer service should be the foundation of every painting project. What started as a small, family-run operation has grown into one of Maple Grove's most trusted painting companies.

With over 15 years of experience, our team has completed hundreds of residential painting projects across the Twin Cities. We've built our reputation one satisfied customer at a time, earning trust through consistent quality and professional service.

Today, we remain committed to the same principles that got us started: meticulous attention to detail, respect for our clients' homes, and a dedication to delivering exceptional results.

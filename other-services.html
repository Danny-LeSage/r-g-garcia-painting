Highlighted Other Work

A few of the additional services we are often asked about.

Power Washing

Surface cleaning that helps exterior areas look better and prepares siding, trim, decks, and other spaces for seasonal upkeep or future paint work.

Christmas Light Installation

Seasonal help for homeowners who want a clean, polished holiday look without taking on the installation themselves.

Floor Refinishing

Refinishing support that helps worn floors feel cleaner, brighter, and more in step with the rest of the home.

Not Sure If We Handle It?

If your project is related to finishing, exterior cleanup, or another residential improvement need, send us a message. We can let you know if it is a service we offer or whether there is a better next step.

Best Way to Ask

  • Tell us what the project is and what kind of help you need
  • Share where it is located on the home or property
  • Let us know if you already have a rough timeline in mind
  • Ask even if you are not sure it fits one of our main service pages

Helpful Details

  • Photos of the area or surfaces you want us to look at
  • Room, exterior, or project descriptions with a little context
  • Approximate square footage or size if you know it
  • Any finish goals, concerns, or ideas you already have in mind
